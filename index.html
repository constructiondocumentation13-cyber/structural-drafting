Our Core Offerings

Comprehensive Structural Drafting Solutions

Equipping your construction and engineering teams with the precise 2D and 3D documentation needed to bring complex architectures to life safely.

Structural 2D Drafting

Accurate and detailed 2D drafting for residential, commercial, and industrial structures. We convert architectural designs into ready-to-use digital blueprints.

Steel Detailing

Expert preparation of steel shop drawings, fabrication drawings, and erection layouts ensuring flawless onsite assembly and structural integrity.

Rebar Detailing

Comprehensive reinforced concrete detailing, including slab details, beam intersections, and joint placements for maximum structural reinforcement.

Structural 3D Modeling

Advanced 3D modeling services providing spatial clarity, clash detection, and accurate bill of materials (BOM) before the construction phase begins.

Foundation Plan Drawings

Precise drafting for shallow, raft, and pile foundations, ensuring the critical base of your structure is planned to exact engineering specifications.

As-Built Drawings

Updating structural drafts post-construction to reflect the final, existing conditions of the building for facility management and future renovations.
